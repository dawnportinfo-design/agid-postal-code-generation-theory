# 2. Model Taxonomy

The repository groups the requested models into six families.

## A. Core Partition and Coding Models

- minimum postal-zone count;
- collision avoidance;
- variable-length expansion;
- information-theoretic code capacity;
- typo tolerance;
- search complexity.

## B. Geography and Topology Models

- islands and exclaves;
- ferry and remote connectivity;
- terrain constraints;
- latitude/longitude to postal zone;
- high-rise and complex facility subdivision.

## C. Administrative and Lifecycle Models

- administrative boundary change;
- future population change;
- new city development;
- disaster reconfiguration;
- postal-code lifetime optimization.

## D. Operational Delivery Models

- delivery time minimization;
- cost minimization;
- carrier load balancing;
- parcel volume balance;
- EC demand optimization;
- road-network partitioning.

## E. Weak or Missing Postal-Code Models

- no-postal-code country generation;
- insufficient-code redesign;
- virtual town ambiguity resolution;
- AddressGrid fallback;
- AGID-integrated postal equivalent zones.

## F. Global Compatibility Models

- international address compatibility;
- carrier-neutral numbering;
- administrative and private-sector coexistence;
- virtual address space;
- global common encoding.
